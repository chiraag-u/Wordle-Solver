What is Wordle?

Wordle is a popular online word puzzle game where players have six tries to guess a five-letter word. Each guess must be a valid word, and the game provides feedback on the guesses by indicating which letters are in the correct position, which are in the word but the wrong position, and which are not in the word at all. It's a fun and addictive way to challenge your vocabulary and deduction skills!
Check out at https://www.nytimes.com/games/wordle/
